The MineChat of the future

Are you tired of having to keep your phone on you whenever you want to chat in Minecraft? Has your computer been unable to connect to a server and you want to join the fun? Now introducing: WMcChat2

What is it?

WMcChat2 (Web Minecraft Chat) is a web-based Minecraft chat client, similar to MineChat. It is based off of it's original project (WMcChat), however it now uses Node.JS instead of Java
